Viola Davis

The Importance of Viola Davis as an Artist

  • She is one of only two African American women who have gotten Academy Award nominations for both Lead Actress and Supporting Actress. The only other woman to achieve that accomplishment is Whoopi Goldberg.
  • Davis made Emmy history in 2015 when she became the first African American woman to win the award for Lead Actress in a Drama Series.
  • In 2017, Davis received her third Oscar nomination for acting. And with that, she became the first African American woman to receive three Oscar nominations in her career.
